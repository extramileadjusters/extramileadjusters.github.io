FAQ

Common Questions

What types of estimates do you offer?

We offer both in-person field estimates and remote photo estimates, so you can choose the option that fits your shop's workflow and timeline.

How fast will I hear back after signing up?

We aim to contact every new shop within minutes of receiving your form submission during business hours.

Do I need to use CCC to sign up?

No — CCC is just one platform we work with. We'll discuss compatibility during your onboarding call.

Is there a contract or minimum commitment?

No contracts and no minimums. You use Extra Mile Adjusters when you need us.

How does the 25% discount work?

New body shop accounts receive 25% off the cost of their first three estimates. The discount is applied automatically to your account when you sign up during our early access period.

I'm an adjuster — what carriers will I work with?

Our adjusters work across all major carriers including State Farm, GEICO, Allstate, Progressive, USAA, Farmers, Liberty Mutual, Nationwide, Travelers, AAA, and more.
