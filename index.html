The Infrastructure Gap is the Opportunity

Auto body shops are forced to manage adjuster relationships manually — cold calls, disconnected scheduling, paper estimates. Carriers face unpredictable cycle times and quality variance.

Extra Mile Adjusters eliminates this friction with a dispatch platform that serves all three stakeholders simultaneously: body shops get faster turnaround, adjusters get consistent volume, carriers get standardized data.

  • Photo estimates reduce total cycle time by 4–6 days on average
  • Field adjuster dispatch from submission to confirmation in under 2 hours
  • CCC-native workflow means zero change management for existing shops
  • All major carrier integrations pre-built — no custom development required
  • Recurring revenue model: shops pay per estimate, adjusters earn per assignment

FAQ

Common Questions

What estimate types do you offer?

Both in-person field estimates and remote photo estimates. Choose the option that fits your shop's workflow and timeline — or use both depending on the job.

How quickly will I hear back after signing up?

We contact every new shop within minutes of receiving a form submission during business hours. No automated queues.

Do I need to use CCC?

No — CCC is the most common platform we integrate with, but compatibility is discussed during onboarding. We work with all major estimating tools.

Is there a contract or minimum volume?

No contracts, no minimums. Use Extra Mile when you need it. The platform scales with your shop's volume.

How does the 25% early access discount work?

Shops that join during early access receive 25% off their first three estimates. Applied automatically — no codes, no friction.

What carriers do your adjusters work with?

All major carriers: State Farm, GEICO, Allstate, Progressive, USAA, Farmers, Liberty Mutual, Nationwide, Travelers, AAA, and 22 others.
