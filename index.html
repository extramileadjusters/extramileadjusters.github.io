Common Questions

We've Heard Every Concern.

What shops ask us

Q
What if I already have adjuster relationships?

Keep them. Extra Mile is a backup and overflow system — not a replacement. When your regular adjuster is booked, we're ready. Most shops use both.

Q
We use Mitchell / Audatex, not CCC. Will it work?

Yes. CCC is our deepest integration, but we work with all major estimating platforms. Your onboarding contact will confirm compatibility before you submit your first estimate.

Q
What if an adjuster's work quality is poor?

Every adjuster has a quality score based on carrier feedback and shop ratings. Poor performers are removed. If you ever receive a substandard estimate, we make it right — same day.

What adjusters ask us

Q
How quickly do I actually get paid?

Net-7 from assignment delivery. No net-30, no "processing delays." We built our billing cycle around what adjusters actually need, not what's convenient for us.

Q
Will I have consistent volume or just busy periods?

As more shops join the network in your area, your volume compounds. Early network adjusters in active markets are seeing a full calendar within 60–90 days.

Q
Do I have to take every assignment dispatched to me?

No. You set your availability and coverage area. Assignments are offered, not mandated. You control your schedule — we just keep the queue full.

FAQ

Common Questions

What estimate types do you offer?

Both in-person field estimates and remote photo estimates. Choose the option that fits your shop's workflow and timeline — or use both depending on the job.

How quickly will I hear back after signing up?

We contact every new shop within minutes of receiving a form submission during business hours. A real person — not an automated sequence.

Do I need to use CCC?

No. CCC is our deepest integration, but we work with Mitchell, Audatex, and all major estimating tools. Compatibility is confirmed during onboarding.

Is there a contract or minimum volume?

No contracts, no minimums. Use Extra Mile when you need it. The platform scales with your shop's volume — nothing to cancel.

How does the 25% early access discount work?

Shops that join during early access receive 25% off their first three estimates. Applied automatically at the time of billing — no codes, no friction.

What carriers do your adjusters work with?

All major carriers: State Farm, GEICO, Allstate, Progressive, USAA, Farmers, Liberty Mutual, Nationwide, Travelers, AAA, and 22 others. Carrier relationships are pre-built — no qualifying required from your shop.

For adjusters: how does payment work?

Net-7 from assignment delivery. Every time. We don't run net-30 or conditional payment schedules. Assignments are tracked, deliveries are logged, payment goes out on schedule.

For adjusters: can I control my availability?

Yes. You set your coverage area and availability. Assignments are offered to you — you don't have to accept everything. Your calendar, your control.
